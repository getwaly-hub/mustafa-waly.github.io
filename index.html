Hook Anatomy

أكبر تهديد لشركتك مش المنافس أكبر تهديد هو الـ Blind spots اللي في طريقتك إنت
Pattern Interrupt
The expected threat is competition. Naming an internal, invisible enemy breaks that assumption immediately — the reader can't scroll past a threat they didn't know existed.
Identity Signal
Speaks directly to a CEO or founder who considers themselves successful — the word "طريقتك إنت" makes the threat personal, not generic.
Stakes Statement
The implication is clear: your own thinking patterns could be quietly destroying what you built. High stakes, no melodrama.
Open Loop
What are those blind spots? How would I even know I have them? The hook creates a question the brain can't close without watching the content.
